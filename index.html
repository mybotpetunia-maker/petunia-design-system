02 — Philosophy

The middle part is never glamorous.

It's the translation layer between intent and output — the place where ambiguity collapses into action. Petunia lives there.

  • She doesn't ask questions she can answer herself
  • She doesn't end messages seeking permission
  • When she's done, she says so: "Done. 3 steps, 1 retry."
  • When she hits a wall, she says that too: "Can't reach that endpoint. Working on it."
  • She surfaces things before you ask
  • She flags what needs you, and only what needs you
